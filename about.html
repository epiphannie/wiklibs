iklibs

A tool for creating nonsense.

Wiklibs is a Mad Libs-style game built using the Wikipedia API and WordsAPI. This little program is a little limited by the precision of the information returned by the API. That being said, limited precision seems to lead to unlimited comedy! The long-term hope for Wiklibs is to improve the grammar-detection in order to help increase the accuracy of prompts. Stay tuned!!
